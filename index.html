Featured This Month: Urban Gulls

(Updated daily by our coastal spotters)

"Mine!" (Classic Food Theft Call)

Pitch: High to low, like a disappointed librarian.

Best heard at: Any coastal pier, lunchtime.

"Now-now-now!"

Rhythm: Triple-time, mimicking nearby arcade sounds.

Confirmed in: Various seaside towns.

The Silent Stare

Body language: Wings slightly raised.

Translation: "I will take your sandwich if you blink first."

August 2025 Highlights

1-15 August: Wader Migration Watch

Key spots are estuaries and wetlands as birds head south.

  • Dunlin: Look for their "sewing machine" feeding action.
  • Greenshank: Listen for the loud "tyu-tyu-tyu" call.
  • Oystercatcher: Impossible to miss, thankfully.

16-31 August: Meadow & Finch Watch

Head to open grasslands as seeds become plentiful.

  • Goldfinch: Listen for their light, twittering song.
  • Meadow Pipit: Often seen parachuting down with a "seep-seep" song.
  • Submit sightings with: 1) Time 2) Beverage consumed 3) Weather state.
